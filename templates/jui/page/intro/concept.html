Introduction to Budgets

To understand how development is done in Agile Technologies, it is important to understand a concept of a "Budget". We have made some fancy images to explain it.

Projects

Projects can be long and never-ending. It can span acros multiple milestones and consist of many tasks. Multiple vendors could be working on larger projects.

Budget

Budget is a part of project which is limited in 3 ways: functionality, timeline and cost.

Budget-based tracking

We use a budget-based tracking. Each budget is separatelly estimated and quoted. When new budget is allocated its decided: when budget ends, what are the conditions for closing budget and what payment is used for the budget.
