What is Colubris?

At Agile we treat Web Development very seriously.

That's why we have developed a whole web application to keep control on our projects and empower our clients to see all the important aspects of the project:

  • Agreed project specification
  • Financial arrangement and involved team members
  • Planend and actual timeline

How to get access / become a client?

Colubris at project.agiletech.ie is accessible to only to existing and potential clients of Agile Technoolgies. Once you place your quote with us, you will be sent an invitation to your account in Colubris. There you will be able to find your Quote breakdown as well as will be able to interract with our team through a number of useful tools.

Agile Technologies specialize in development Web Software based on Agile Toolkit and interface similar to this one. Your web interface could look professional yet remain simple and affordable.

Security and Privacy

Information you publish or information made available to you through Colubris can only be seen by you or employees/subcontractors of Agile Technologies who have signed NDA and contract with our company.

Download Colubris to use it on your site.

If you are a web developer, designer or web consultancy company, by using "Colubris" you can organize your projects and engage with your clients. Colubris is available to download free of charge under open-source license. It is a great testament and example to our software development skills and passion for Open-Source at Agile Technologies.

Colubris can be used by development team and software managers for any type of contract-basis software development. The main difference with other project tracking software packages, is that Colubris focuses on interaction between client and development team. of the project and works best with quote-based 1-4 month projects delivered by your team or sub-contractors.
